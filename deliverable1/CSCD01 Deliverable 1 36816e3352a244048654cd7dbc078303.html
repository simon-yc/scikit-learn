Methods of Communication

The primary method of communication is through our personal CSCD01 Discord server and the secondary method is through email.

  • Discord response time: within 24 hours
  • Email response time: 24 hours

Meeting Attendance

  • Primary Tutorial: TUT0001 (Thurs. 15:00 - 17:00)
  • Required weekly meetings (Discord Server):
    • Saturday @ 18:00-18:30
    • Wednesday @ 18:00-18:30
  • Meetings will take place through the CSCD01 Discord server in the General Voice channel
  • All standup meetings are mandatory unless otherwise mentioned in the Discord server
  • Additional meetings will be scheduled as necessary and after approval from all team members

Meeting Preparation

  • All meetings will be done online and no in-person meetings will be scheduled
  • Each member will be given adequate time in round robin order to share their updates and statuses on their assigned tasks

Division of work

  • All team members will receive a fair and balanced workload
  • All tasks will follow the self-assignment methodology where users decide which tasks they wish to work on
  • Tasks should be distributed fairly among users, so that each person contributes a similar amount of effort when possible.
  • If no team member volunteers for a task, a meeting will occur and the task will be assigned to the most appropriate person.

Submitting Assignments

  • To ensure timely submissions to the main branch, a 24-hour code freeze will be implemented on the repository before significant deadlines
  • All contributions must be made 24 hours prior to the deadline
  • All pushes to the dev branch must be reviewed by a team member before being merged
  • It is the responsibility of each team member to ensure their submissions meet the standards and are pushed to the repository

Contingency Planning

  • If a team member drops out of the course their workload will be equally distributed among the remaining members
  • If a team member misses multiple meetings a one-to-one meeting will be held with the individual
  • If the situation gets worse it will be raised to the attention of a T.A or the professor
  • If a team member is academically dishonest it will be immediately brought to the attention of the professor
  • Any other situation will be assessed on a case by case basis
